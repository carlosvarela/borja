EmpathyBroker Commerce Search is the engine that powers top retailers online (Zara, Dutti, Mango, Adoreme or Mediamarkt among others). EmpathyBroker is for all those who believe on teamwork, commitment, innovation and excellence.

We are looking for hardworking, enthusiastic and open minded people, but especially proactive and eager to learn.

Position Summary

You will provide visualisation to make complex information and data sets understandable through rich visual formats that are insightful and actionable.

Work youâ€™ll do

Data Viz engineer work within an engagement front team.

Key responsibilities will include:

  • Function as integrators between business needs and technology solutions, helping to create new visualisations base on search concepts.
  • Defining systems strategy, developing system requirements, designing, prototyping, and testing custom technology solutions, and supporting system implementation.

The Team

Layer front team

At EmpathyBroker, our Layer/Front team is responsible of offering a front experience to Empathy Search.

Play Team

At EmpathyBroker, our Play team is responsible of transforming data into actionable information for our clients.

Required Qualifications:

  • The developer should have three or more years of experience designing, building, and implementing presentation services.
  • Experience with JQuery, AngularJS, or similar GUI libraries.
  • Experience with D3.js, Raphael.js, or comparable data visualisation front.
  • Modern Web-development technologies, including ES6, JavaScript, AJAX, HTML5, CSS, etc.
  • Team collaboration tools, such as Git, Bitbucket, Jira, Confluence, etc.
  • Interfacing with RESTful Web Services.

Preferred:

  • Experience with developing geospatial visualisations.
  • Ability to drive insight by designing visualisations with logical and meaningful data flow.
  • Expertise in building data visualisations with a strong understanding of the appropriate chart types to use to highlight patterns in the data for unstructured and structured data sets.
  • Experience in data modeling, analysis, design, testing, development and implementation.
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple task assignments.
  • Strong oral and written communication skills.
  • An advanced degree in specialisation is preferred.

If you hold these skills, contact us.
