Would you like to understand what the impact of the size of your search box has on the search experience on your site? At EmpathyBroker we are working on the analysis of this and are looking to answer questions as:

  • Do more visitors use search depending on the size of the search box?
  • Is the impact different for new than for repeat visitors?
  • Is the size of the search box correlated to the number of searches per session for repeat visitors?
  • Does it impact the results viewed per search?
  • Does it have an impact on the finding journey?
  • What are the differences between Desktop and Mobile?
If you are an eCommerce and you are interested in participating and receiving the results, please drop me an email at barbaram@empathybroker.com. We will need some data from you, which our team will help pulling. All the data we receive will not be shared publicly, only the results of the analysis. The only requirements to participate are to be an eCommerce, and to be looking forward to getting the insightful results of the study!
