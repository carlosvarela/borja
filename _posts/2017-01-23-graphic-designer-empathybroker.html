EmpathyBroker Commerce Search is the engine that powers on top retailers online (Dutti, Mango, Adoreme or Mediamarkt)

EmpathyBroker is for all those who believe on teamwork, commitment, innovation and excellence.
We are looking for hardworking, enthusiastic and open minded people, but especially proactive and eager to learn.
You would enjoy an amazing work environment, with the best equipment and many other social benefits. The place of work could be either Gijón or Ferrol

Requirements
• Portfolio examples to show hands-on experience on design projects.

Qualifications

Minimum qualifications:
• Experience with Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign
• Experience with HTML5, CSS3

Preferred qualifications:
• Experience in front end development, knowledge on JavaScript

If you hold these skills and are interested in this challenge contact us.
