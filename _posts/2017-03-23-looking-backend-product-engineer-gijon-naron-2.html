On EmpathyBroker we work daily to reduce the distance between products / catalog and users by search engines that connect to all kinds of needs.
This company is for all those who trust on teamwork, commitment, innovation and quality service.

We are looking for hardworking, enthusiastic and open minded people, but especially proactive and full of eager to learn.

What do we expect from you as a team member?
- Willing to learn and try new stuff/tools
- No fear to say "I don't know"
- To be positive and open minded
- To be customer focused: customers is what we care about the most

What do we expect from you as an engineer?
- To be proficient with Java8 or Python3+ (another languages such as Go are welcome as well)
- To be a documentation writer! We like to share what we do with our team members
- Knowledge about REST APIs development and interface design
- Liking to learn distributed systems development

What will we learn together if you work with us?
- distributed systems development problems and how to evaluate trade-offs
- Amazing systems such as Zookeeper, Consul, Solr et al.
- Task automation! Repeating the same task manually every day is boring, isn't it?

What do you get if you work with us?
- Good computer gear: MacBook Pro 13' Retina and 24' display.
- Competent salary above market
- Be part of this awesome adventure about creating our own product
- Attendance to worldwide technical events
- An unlimited supply of technical (and non-tech) books

If you hold these skills and are interested in this challenge contact us.
