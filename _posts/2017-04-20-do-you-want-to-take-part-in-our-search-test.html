At EmpathyBroker we are defining what Search Happiness is. For that we are running remote user tests in order to understand the way people search.

What do we need from you?

Just a few of your minutes to enter our demo and do a couple of tasks.

What do you get from this?

You can win a trip to London or to Barcelona for a weekend, depending if you are participating in the Spanish or the English version.

Is there right or wrong on the test?

No, there is no right or wrong. We all search and browse different.

That easy, really?

Yes, that easy :-)

You can participate until the end of this month. Here you can enter our demo:
https://goo.gl/7HRn2g

Leave a message or email me if you have any questions.
