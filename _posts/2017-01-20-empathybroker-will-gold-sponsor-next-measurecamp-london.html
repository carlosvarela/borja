EmpathyBroker is delighted to announce that will be contributing to the next MeasureCamp London as a Gold sponsor.
 
MeasureCamp is an open and free to attend unconference for the Web Analytics and Digital Marketing industry that had its first edition in London in 2012 and now this city is getting ready for the 10th one. It has been 4 and a half years since the first MeasureCamp took place and during these years, the unconference has been held in other cities around the world like Paris, Hong Kong, Barcelona, Amsterdam, Madrid, Melbourne, and many others.
 
From EmpathyBroker we work every day in improving the Search Happiness. Please come see us to our stand in MeasureCamp to talk about the exciting topic of happiness in search.
 
EmpathyBroker is the Search & Navigation platform for Sites and Apps that powers Worldwide online stores such as Zara, Mango, Monsoon, Soho Home, Adoreme and Spares, among others.
